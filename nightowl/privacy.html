Privacy Policy for NightOwl Discord Bot

Last updated: 11th october 2024

1. Introduction

NightOwl is a Discord bot based on RedBot (https://github.com/Cog-Creators/Red-DiscordBot), which uses plugins for its functions. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use NightOwl.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We collect and use data solely for the purpose of providing and improving NightOwl's functions. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Server IDs
  • Command usage data
  • Role information
  • Message content (for moderation and command processing)

The data collected is used exclusively for the bot's functions to work properly, including moderation, ticket system, trivia games, custom commands, and other features provided by RedBot and its plugins.

3. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored securely and is only retained as long as necessary for the bot's functionality or as required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

NightOwl may use third-party services provided by RedBot plugins. The use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Discord Terms of Service

This privacy policy does not supersede the Discord Terms of Service. Users are encouraged to review Discord's terms as well.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time.

7. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or NightOwl's data practices, please join AliceD's support server: https://discord.gg/7peUVemWgS

8. Acceptance of Policy

By using NightOwl, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy policy and agree to its terms.
